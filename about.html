Hi! I'm Anu, and thank you for visiting my store. Since you're here, let me tell you a little bit about myself and my knitting journey.

Born and brought up in India, I picked up knitting in my teenage years. Not only was I inspired by my mother, but I had to knit in school as well (which I obviously enjoyed). Since then, I would knit shawls, scarves, and sweaters for myself and my family members, carrying forward what I learnt throughout the years.

For the past 8 years, I have lived in Singapore. And it was here that my passion for knitting grew. Interested in trying something new, I started knitting hats for charity. After organising multiple meetups, events, and knitting for well over a year, I realized that I wanted explore new horizons.
Wanting to knit something more complex, I joined my first test knit. And frankly, I loved it. Test knitting allows me to try out new patterns and designs while making a real impact to the knitting community. I mostly test knit shawls, of which you can see many pictures on my Instagram. If you'd like me to test knit something for you, feel free to contact me.

At the current moment, I am still test knitting frequently. However, I have too many shawls to possibly use myself, so I've decided to start selling a few on my store here. If you're looking for something to complement your outfits or want to give someone a thoughtful gift, these shawls can really be wonderful.

Once again, thanks for visiting my site, and I hope you have a great day. Happy shopping!

- Anuradha Mukherjee
